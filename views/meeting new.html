Make An Appointment

Here you can send a request in order to meet with a volunteer.

The volunteer can help you cope with the difficulties of absorption in Israel.
All you have to do is to fill in the reason for the meeting, date, time and place where you want to make the meeting.
Your request will be sent to all the volunteers and when the first volunteer will accept your request, you will be notified in the confirmation of the meeting.
If the meeting has been approved and you can not get there you must inform the volunteer and to delete the request from the list of request on your profile page.
